Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish that exemplifies simplicity and richness in flavor. Originating from Rome, this dish features spaghetti tossed in a creamy sauce made from eggs, Pecorino Romano cheese, and crispy pancetta or guanciale. The sauce's silky texture is achieved without cream, relying instead on the heat of the pasta to create a smooth, velvety coating.
